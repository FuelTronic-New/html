Xero Invoice Setup

Adding a Payment Service

To get paid quickly and easily, add a payment service to your invoices to allow easy online payment for your customers.

The following steps will help you add a payment service:
  1. Login to your Xero company by clicking here
  2. Choose the company that has been linked on the Xero API screen.
  3. Click on the Settings menu option, then choose General Settings.
  4. On the General Settings page under the Features heading, find and click on Invoice settings or follow this link : Invoice settings.

  5. At the top of the Invoice settings Page click on Payment services.
  6. On the Payment services page click on the "+Add Payment Services" button, then choose Custom Payment URL.

  7. Here is a quick link to Payment Services
  8. In the Name field fill in "ECI Payment Gateway"
  9. In the Your Custom URL copy and paste the URL below:
    {{data.payment_url}}
  10. Then click Save.

Adding the Payment Gateway to a Invoice Template

The following steps will help you add the "ECI Payment Gateway" to one of your invoice templates:
  1. Above the Payment Services Heading choose Invoice Settings.
  2. Select options on any of your Invoice Templates, then choose edit.
  3. On the Edit Branding Theme Modal find the Payment Services option in the middle column, select "ECI Payment Gateway" from the drop down.

  4. Now click save.
Once configured, you can now raise and email invoices. The link in received emails will navigate to an online Xero invoice with a ‘Pay now’ button.

When clients make use of the ‘Pay now’ button this portal will handle the payment via Eci, and if the payment is successful the portal will create a receipt via the Xero API using the Account number that has been selected on the Xero API tab.

When funds show up in your bank feed reconcile it to the same account.
